Privacy Policy

At Stress Sense, your privacy is our top priority. We only collect essential data to personalize your stress management experience and improve the accuracy of our AI predictions. We are committed to protecting your information and ensuring your trust in our platform.

All data you provide, including stress inputs and profile information, is securely stored and never shared with third-party services without your explicit consent. We use industry-standard encryption techniques to safeguard your personal information.

You are in full control of your data. You can view, update, or delete your account and data at any time. Our mission is to support your mental well-being while maintaining complete transparency and privacy in how your information is handled.
